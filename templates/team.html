Mission Statement

The creators of the Supreme Buddy Buddy brings to you a site that helps interact with other Brandeisians who have similar interest in familiar settings at Brandeis. Essentially the way the website functions is that it display different categories concerning things around campus. Our categories include: GYM, ACADEMICS, and FOOD. The aforementioned categories will then contain subcategories in those areas of interest. For example, the GYM category will have subcategories of different locations you can go to like [The weight room, Sport Courts, Pool, and Track]
